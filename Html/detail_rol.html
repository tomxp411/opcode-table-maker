65C02 Opcode Details: ROL

Rotate Left

SYNTAX MODE HEX LEN CYCLES FLAGS
ROL A Accumulator $2A 1 2 CZ----N
ROL $12 Zero Page $26 2 5 CZ----N
ROL $12,X Zero Page,X $36 2 6 CZ----N
ROL $1234 Absolute $2E 3 6 CZ----N
ROL $1234,X Absolute,X $3E 3 6/7 CZ----N +p

Rotate all bits to the left one position. The value in the carry (C) flag is shifted into bit 0 and the original bit 7 is shifted into the carry (C).

[ASL](#asl) rotates _through_ the Carry flag, effectively making a 9 bit shift.
[ROR](#ror) rotates to the right

+p add one cycle when addr + x crosses a page boundary.
