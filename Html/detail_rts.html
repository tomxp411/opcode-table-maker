65C02 Opcode Details: RTS

Return from Subroutine

SYNTAX MODE HEX LEN CYCLES FLAGS
RTS Implied $60 1 6 -------

Typically used at the end of a subroutine. It jumps back to the address after the [JSR](#jsr) that called it by popping the top 2 bytes off the stack and transferring control to that address +1.
